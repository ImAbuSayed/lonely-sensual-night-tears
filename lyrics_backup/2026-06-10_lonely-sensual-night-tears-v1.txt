[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The porch light flickers in the southern breeze
Your heavy eyes are calling out to me
We're wrapped inside the shadows of the trees
And I am drifting on your quiet sea

[Verse 1]
The headlights fade across the dusty floor
I hear the steady rhythm of your breath
I never wanted anything so much before
I’ll hold you closely till there’s nothing left

[Pre-Chorus]
The fire is burning with a steady glow
My trembling hands are reaching out for yours
I want to take it beautifully slow
And lock away the world outside these doors

[Chorus]
These lonely sensual night tears begin to fall
When you are looking deeply into me
I hear the quiet answering my call
You are the only truth I want to see

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, the midnight is slowly breaking
[Whispered] (And my soul is waking)

[Verse 2]
I trace the gentle curve along your spine
While summer rain is tapping on the glass

And never let the sweet illusion pass
